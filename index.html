About Me

Hi, I am Divya! I am a professional calligrapher based in the San Fransico Bay Area. My insatiable curiosity to dabble in new skills transformed me from an engineer to a calligrapher. I fell in love with calligraphy! So I spent countless days practicing and honing the new skill, learning something new each day. I wish to keep penmanship alive and help pass my love for calligraphy and arts to others by offering resources to help people learn this timeless craft! My belief is that it is NEVER too late to learn something new ❤️
